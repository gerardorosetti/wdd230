Target Audience

The target audience are owners, investors, individuals, groups, etc.. that are interested in expanding or getting a better performance in their business through generating a better relationship with clients and that are looking for more clients in the future. Also, we are looking to help owners and investors to form partnerships.

Personas

Jesus Salas is a 22-year-old young student who is starting and leading his new business with his family. They are looking to make a new home products store well and centered on the city. He is looking for some recyclable companies and donators of recyclable content to make new and well done products by recycling. Also, they work with mirrors and crystal-personalized works.

Alejandro Mujica is a 35-year-old man who is starting a game development business. He is looking mostly for young talent to come into the project. The plan is to develop a PC game using the Unreal Engine 4.

Scenarios

Jesus wants to meet other business owners and potential customers.
Alejandro is looking for pationated people that is interested into start the new business.
Jesus wants to learn more about marketing strategies to attract more customers to her boutique.
Alejandro wants to get some new technical resources so the team can develop and use with property the engine.
