We are Bountiful Foods

We are a small business specializied in organic, locally grown foods. We are physically located in Carlsbad, California, but do not worry, we have many food trucks that cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Diego. The fruits we sell are produce directly from the farmers in the area, but also uses some national and international suppliers. You can get our products and make your order using your phone, laptop or desktop using our website. Our main mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas. And finally our theme is to support healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience
